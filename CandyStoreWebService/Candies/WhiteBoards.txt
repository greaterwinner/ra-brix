This module is a highly flexible and rich module for being able to create advanced lists which you can use for anything you wish. If any of your "tracking problems" are "listible problems", then this module will highly likely solve these problems. It could be used fo creating lists to track your CD collections, tasks or todos, employees and basically anything that a problem related to "lists". It's highly flexible and will make you able to create searchable, sortable lists of very high complexity.
